Who am I?

My name is Bobby Crawford. I am a gamer, a weightlifter, a scub driver, and a Warhammer 40k hobbyist.
I first got in to the Warhammer 40k not by playing the miniatures but by reading the Warhammer 40k novels
about 20 years ago. About 2 years ago when I had the funds and the confidence, I decided to try out the miniature game.
I now have a 3,000 point Dark Angel army and just started 1,250 point Adeptus Mechanicus.
