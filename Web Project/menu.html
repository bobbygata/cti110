"In the grim darkness of the 41st Millennium, there is only war."

Warhammer 40,000 is a tabletop miniature wargame produced by Games Workshop, that is set in a
dystopain science fanstay universe. The majority of the human race are called Imperium Of Man, is the dominant
but dwindling force in the galaxy. The Imperium of Man is constantly at war with the forces of Choas, Xenos and sometimes themselves.
