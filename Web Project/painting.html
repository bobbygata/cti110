Tips and tricks

This page is going to be about painting tips and tricks that will be useful for beginners.

Tips #1

When painting your miniatures, it's always best to thin down your paints and applied
two thin coats vs. one thick coat. By thinning down your paints, you will get a smoothier coat and
you will be able to see the details.

Tricks #1

I found that holding the miniature by the base for long period of time can put a strain on your hand.
By using ble tack and aerosol cap, or something similar, can lessen hand strain while greatly increase your
grip on the miniature.
